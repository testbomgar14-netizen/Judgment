RULES & GUIDE
About the Game
Kachuful (also known as Judgement or Oh Hell) is a trick-taking card game where the challenge is not just winning hands — but accurately predicting how many tricks you will win.
Round Order & Trump Suits
♠R1 & R9 — Spades: Spades are trump
♦R2 & R8 — Diamonds: Diamonds are trump
♣R3 & R7 — Clubs: Clubs are trump
♥R4 & R6 — Hearts: Hearts are trump
🚫R5 & R10 — No Trump: No trump suit this round
Core Rules
🎯The Goal: Win the exact number of tricks you bid. More or fewer = zero points.
🃏The Play: You must follow the lead suit if you have it. If not, play a trump to win or discard.
👑Trump: The declared trump suit beats all other suits in that round.
Scoring
✅Correct bid: 10 + tricks won  e.g. bid 2, win 2 = 12 pts
❌Wrong bid: 0 pts — even one trick off and you score nothing.
Winning Strategy
💡Bid Low if Unsure: Conservative bids are safer — 0 is a valid bid!
👁Track Trumps: Watch how many trumps have been played to know when your cards are safe.
0️⃣Nil Bid: Bidding zero on a weak hand earns a steady 10 points if you win nothing.
